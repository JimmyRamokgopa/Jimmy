About Jimmy

Jimmy graduated from the University of the Witwatersrand (Johannesburg) in 2014, holding a Bachelor of Science Degree in Engineering (Civil). He is a social activist, creative, writer and a professional in his field of operational management. His interests are in media, social justice, politics and socio-economics.

VISION:       Collaboration. Prosperity. Unity.
